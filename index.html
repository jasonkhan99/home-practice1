Welcome to Jason's Movie Collection!

A database to list, sort and categorize my ungodly film library.

I first fell in love with cinema my senior year of high school in 2004. A friend of mine took me to the New Beverly Cinema to see a double feature of Band A Part and Un Femme est une Femme by Jean Luc Goddard. It was then I knew that I loved movies outside of the mainstream and soon after started amassing the collection detailed on this website...
